A Unit for Promotion of Art & Culture.

StAR (Supporting the Artistic Radiance) is an organization whose sole purpose is to cream the milk of brilliance and various skills that's amassed in the soul of a student, and further nurture and grow the vast furrows of art and culture.Beneath the roof of StAR, they have a Literary club, Fine Art club, Music club, Dance club, Yoga club, Drama club, and Personality Development club. Through these coterie, they aim to sieve, and bring out the abundance of talent inside a pupil in the best possible way.
This organization will be run by a capable, experienced and accomplished set of students, who're passionate about preserving and encouraging the ancient race of art birthed by various cultural influences. And they will take their very first steps from those schools, who by some reason or the other, have remained unable to provide facilities to develop their student's artistic skills.The motive of StAR is to always be there, and do the best that they can do, for the student's development, and for the benefit of our society and nation.
